## Formation
1. As long as this creature is attacking with another creature with formation, it’s in formation. Creatures in formation have trample and base toughness equal to the combined toughness of creatures in formation.
2. As long as this creature is attacking with another creature with formation, it’s in formation. Creatures in formation have trample and +1/+X, where X is the total number of creatures in formation.
3. As long as this creature is attacking with another creature with formation, it’s in formation. Creatures in formation have trample and get +1/+1 for each other creature in formation.
