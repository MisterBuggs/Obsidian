## Formation
1. As long as this creature is attacking with another creature with formation, it’s in formation. Creatures in formation have trample and base toughness equal to the combined toughness of creatures in formation.
2. As long as this creature is attacking with another creature with formation, it’s in formation. Creatures in formation have trample and +1/+X, where X is the total number of creatures in formation.
3. As long as this creature is attacking with another creature with formation, it’s in formation. Creatures in formation have trample and get +1/+1 for each other creature in formation.
4. At the begin of combat, you may tap two or more creatures with with this keyword. They are considered in formation. The formation counts as a creature of base power and toughness equal to the combined power and toughness of the creatures in formation +1/+1 for each creature in formation beyond the first. The formation must attack, has trample, and can't be blocked by a number of creatures less than the number of creatures in formation. If the formation dies from taking damage, kill all creatures in formation. If all creatures in formation die before end of combat, kill the formation. 
5. Whenever this creature attacks with one or more other creatures with formation, it gets +1/+1 and gains trample until end of combat. This creature isn’t destroyed by combat damage unless all attacking creatures with formation you control are destroyed.
6. This creature isn’t destroyed by combat damage as long as another attacking creature with formation you control isn’t destroyed.
