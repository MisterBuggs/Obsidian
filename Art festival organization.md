#Random_Idea #Art 
Let the public vote on which artists to invite. Offer salary to the artists based on the share of votes times the art budget. Give curators tents or roofs to present stuff and a split of the art budget. Let them auction the art pieces from the artists. This way, art the artists and curators feel are especially desirable tend to hang more isolated and get more room.
