#Random_Idea #Leisure #History 

While clearly its own unique and highly useful basic material, plastic was developed and adopted so late, that no widespread specialized profession like blacksmithing (metal working, ) carpentry (wood working), or masonry (stone working) sprang from it. Society was already globalized & industrialized enough by the time plastic was adopted that there was no room for small scale local plastic businesses that produce to order to develop. This has slightly bit not majorly reverted by the advent of 3D printers. 

A steam punk / [[plastic punk]] world in which plastic was developed but mineral oil was not discovered would be fun. Instead, people make plastic from whale oil at a small scale.
