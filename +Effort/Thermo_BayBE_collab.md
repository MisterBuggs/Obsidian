TLDR: A custom encoding of the components of common cell culture media and supplements, like DMEM or N2, would be very useful for BayBE cell culture applications.  ^ad1b30

suggest to Thermo fisher / Gibco or to Merck to custom encode their commercial media formulations in a BayBE module to be called via their catalogue numbers as a paid industry academia collaboration. 

Otherwise, it should not be too difficult to scrape their web pages for public formulations and translate them into a big matrix of cat number[rows] / components[columns], concentrations[cells] which can then be called in a custom BayBE package by typing their cat number, similar to custom encoding for media.
