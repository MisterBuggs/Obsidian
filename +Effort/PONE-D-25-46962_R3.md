Reviewer Summary:
In this revision of their primary research manuscript entitled "Integrated single-cell RNA sequencing and Bulk-RNA technologies reveal the immunological characteristics of lactylation related-genes in glioblastoma", Cheng et al. present a secondary analysis of lactate-related genes in published transcriptomics analyses of glioblastoma. Prior comments have been addressed. Most notably, I appreciate the authors opting for a more modest, conservative interpretation in their abstract, which better reflects the actual findings.

3. Overall, the language use in this manuscript is moderate. However, the manuscript remains understandable throughout.
