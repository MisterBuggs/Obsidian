#Art with #People_JMCL #Vacation/Amsterdam
Julian Opie: London sculpture artist playing with blotty line art and the female form. 

Jean-Michel Basquiat: Crayon children's drawing style wall art with surprising internal coherence / glow. 

Icy & Sot: Rather Banky-esque artist duo from Iran. Don't hold up to the master but have some interesting pieces, e.g., *let her be free*. 

Frankey: Happy sculpture art of well-recognizable or pop culture characters. Positive, playful attitude. 

Banksy's art belongs on a wall, not on a wall. 

Six N Five makes decent flowy, hyper-realistic ethereal video art, but like, that's as beautiful and unique as one stage of a video game like Journey and that is interactive and just a one-time video. Why are indie games not in galleries? 

Takashi Murakami: Interesting blend of traditional Japanese, Pop, and street art. 

Overall, one of the most disappointing modern art galleries I've seen. Small collection, uninspired composition / ensemble, very commercial feeling.
