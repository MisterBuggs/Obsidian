#People_Aparna_Sekar #Seller #MHH #Sequencing_RNA [[2026-04-15]]
Singleron offers three potentially interesing technologies:
- Microwell based single cell RNA sequencing for 20000 cells per batch. With facile in house library preparation and a sequencing depth of ca. 30000 reads per cell, this would cost about 2500€ per run.
- Cells can be labeled for up to 10 pre-defined mutant genes per run, and mutations are sequenced, i.e., exact mutation information for the tagged genes, if the mutation is expressed. AML panels and Clonal hematopoiesis panels exist. If a custom panel is required, this costs about 200€ extra per plate, but you have to take at least 16 samples (minimum 18000 €).
- They offer a tracer that is added to cultures for 2h before scRNAseq, enabling the exact differentiation of RNA that was transcribed before or after adding this tracer. This enables much more precise pseudotime estimates / RNAvelocity.
